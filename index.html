About

Heyo! I'm Maria, the mastermind behind this group created to bring the awesome power of extroverts and introverts together. I cant wait to personally welcome you into the group and to help you grow

The dream that keeps my mission alive is to help self proclaimed introverts to shine in front of the group, by sharing their ideas and opinions, and to help self proclaimed extroverts find the joy in actively listening and delving into the deeper aspects of a relationship with members of the group
